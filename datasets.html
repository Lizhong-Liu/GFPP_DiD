Datasets

Our model tries to test the potential impacts of GFPP on mutiple socio-economic indicators: health, human capital (education), and household economic development. Within these three categories, we further specify the variables as following:

Variable Type 1 Health

Source: Physical Fitness (California Department of Education (CDE))

Variable Type 2 School and Demographics

Source: Enrollment & Demographics (CDE)

Variable Type 3 Household Economic Development

Source: School-level Poverty Rate (CDE)

Besides regressional analysis, we also want to examine the potential influence of GFPP on LA's local food business. Unfortunately, for now there is little available micro-level data on it, so we cannot include it in our regression model. Instead, we use American Community Survey data on county level and plot an interactive map to show the growth of different food-related sectors (including Agriculture, Food Services, Retail, and Wholesale). The source of data can be found via this link.
